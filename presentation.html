Article Generation

Claude Sonnet 4 writes 2,000-word SEO articles via the OpenRouter API

What goes in

  • Topic and target keyword
  • Real resort data (prices, distances, slopes)
  • SEO writing rules and format guidelines
  • First-person voice instructions

What comes out

  • Complete Markdown article with frontmatter
  • SEO meta title and description
  • FAQ section for Google snippets
  • Internal links to related articles

SEO Strategy

Following Google's E-E-A-T guidelines

Built for Google

E-E-A-T Signals

  • Experience -- First-person writing style
  • Expertise -- Real resort data and facts
  • Authority -- Author bio on every page
  • Trust -- About page, dates, schema

Technical SEO

  • JSON-LD schema (BlogPosting, FAQ, Breadcrumbs)
  • XML sitemap + robots.txt
  • Open Graph + Twitter Cards
  • Internal linking between articles
